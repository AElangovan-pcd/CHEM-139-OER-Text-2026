Useful Conversion Factors

Equivalence

Mass

1 lb = 453.59 g; 1 oz = 28.35 g

Volume

Key Formulas

Concept

Formula

Density

d = m / V

Percentage

% = (part / whole) × 100

Percent error

% error = |measured − true| / true × 100

Percent yield

% yield = (actual / theoretical) × 100

Heat (specific heat)

q = m × c × ΔT

Temperature: F → C

°C = (°F − 32) × 5/9

Temperature: C → F

°F = (°C × 9/5) + 32

Temperature: C → K

K = °C + 273.15

Atomic mass (weighted average)

atomic mass = Σ (isotope mass × fractional abundance)

Moles ↔ mass

moles = mass / molar mass

Moles ↔ particles

moles = particles / NA

Empirical → molecular formula

n = molar mass / empirical formula mass

Stoichiometry road map

mass A → moles A → moles B → mass B
